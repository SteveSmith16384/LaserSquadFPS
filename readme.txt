# LASER SQUAD FPS

A 3D FPS recreation of the first mission of Laser Squad by Julian Gollop.

Programmed by Stephen Smith (stephen.carlylesmith@googlemail.com).  Twitter: @stephencsmith

Gameplay Video: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=R3pW7zwE4YI


## Controls
* W, A, S, D and mouse.
* F - Toggle first person (press 1 & 2 to zoom in and out)
* LMB - Shoot
* Esc - quit


## Running on a Mac
Since the app is not signed for a Mac (I don't even have a Mac), it shows "Application from unidentified developer" on first start.  Open you game with Ctrl+Right Click and then Open.  More details here: https://support.apple.com/en-gb/guide/mac-help/mh40616/mac
